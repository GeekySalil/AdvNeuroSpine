About Our Center

Pioneering Next-Gen
Pain Management.

Located in the heart of Kakarmatta, Varanasi, Advanced Neuro Spine Pain Management Centre is dedicated to eradicating chronic pain and spinal disorders. We are proud to be the pioneers of stitchless, minimally invasive spine endoscopy in North India.

Our philosophy replaces traditional, heavy-cutting surgeries with precision micro-interventions. This means our patients experience significantly less pain, near-zero infection risk, and can often walk home the very same day.

Patient Queries Answered

What exactly is Stitchless Spine Endoscopy?

It is an ultra-minimally invasive surgical technique. Using a high-definition camera and micro-instruments inserted through an opening smaller than a keyhole, we remove the herniated disc causing nerve pressure. It requires no major cutting of muscles or bones, meaning no stitches, minimal bleeding, and much faster recovery.

Can I use my Ayushman Card here?

Yes, absolutely. We are officially empanelled under the Ayushman Bharat PMJAY scheme for major specialties including Orthopaedics (SB), General Surgery (SG), Neurosurgery (SN), Polytrauma (ST), and Urology (SU). We also accept standard cash payments.

Do I need to be put to sleep (General Anesthesia) for procedures?

In many cases, no. Many of our endoscopic and interventional procedures can be performed under local anesthesia. This significantly reduces risks associated with general anesthesia and allows you to communicate with the doctor during the procedure if needed.
